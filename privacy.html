Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

Overview

Arcadia ("we", "our", or "the app") is a game library management and social gaming companion. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile picture URL
  • Google account ID

User-Generated Content

  • Game library entries (games you add, status, ratings, hours played)
  • Reviews you write
  • Friend connections and friend requests
  • Profile information you provide (bio, location, gaming platform IDs)
  • AI-generated roasts and badges you choose to save

Automatically Collected Data

  • OneSignal Player ID (for push notifications)
  • Basic device information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To manage your game library, display your profile, and enable social features
  • AI Recommendations: Your library data is sent to AI services (Groq, Google Gemini) to generate personalized game recommendations and roasts
  • Push Notifications: To notify you of friend requests and other relevant updates
  • Social Features: To connect you with friends and display shared gaming activity

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Appwrite cloud infrastructure
  • Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted SharedPreferences on your device
  • We use HTTPS for all network communications
  • API keys are proxied through secure servers and not embedded in the app

Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely for QR code scanning to add friends. Camera data is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared only:

  • With AI providers (Groq, Gemini) to generate recommendations based on your library
  • With other users according to your privacy settings (profile visibility, friend connections)
  • If required by law

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app's profile and settings
  • Delete your reviews, library entries, and friend connections at any time
  • Request account deletion via our deletion request page or by contacting us at the email below

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Arcadia account and/or specific data at:

Delete Account / Data Request

To process requests and prevent fraud, we may ask you to verify ownership by replying from the same email address used in Arcadia.

Retention: we retain deletion request emails (and any minimal audit trail required to complete the request) for up to 90 days after completion, then delete it.

Children's Privacy

Arcadia is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
