What happens next
Typical processing: up to 30 days

We use your request details only to locate your account and process deletion. We do not use this information for marketing.

  • Full deletion: we delete your Arcadia account and associated app data (subject to technical constraints and legal obligations).
  • Partial deletion: we delete only the items you specify in notes (example: reviews, friend links).
  • Verification: we may ask you to reply from the same email address used in Arcadia.
  • Retention: we retain the request email (and any necessary audit trail) for up to 90 days after completion to prevent fraud and confirm compliance, then delete it.

See our Privacy Policy for details on data handling.
